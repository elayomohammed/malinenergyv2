PIPELINE SECURTY

In the intricate landscape of the oil and gas industry, security is paramount, and Malin Energy, in partnership with OptaSense®, stands as a vanguard in pipeline security expertise. Our Oil & Gas Pipeline Monitoring service is a testament to our commitment to precision, efficiency, and comprehensive threat detection.

Real-time detection is the bedrock of our monitoring service. Leveraging Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) technology, we transform fiber optic cables into real-time threat detectors. This enables us to monitor noise, temperature, pressure, and ground strain with unparalleled accuracy, ensuring that any deviation is detected immediately.

Continuous coverage is a key feature of our system. Installed every 80km, our monitoring stations provide uninterrupted surveillance, rapidly detecting even minor leaks. Our multi-threat detection capabilities go beyond leaks, safeguarding against third-party interference, ground movement, and more, all from a single, integrated platform.

Efficiency and flexibility are inherent in our service. Our enhanced sensitivity allows us to detect smaller leaks ten times faster, with pinpoint location accuracy within 10m. We streamline inspection processes, precisely tracking inline inspection tools for improved maintenance strategies.

Beyond leak detection, we identify and mitigate external threats, ensuring comprehensive security for your pipeline infrastructure. Our use of Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) technology guarantees reliable alarms and customized alert settings, enhancing the overall security posture of your pipelines.

Malin Energy, in collaboration with OptaSense®, safeguards over 15,000km of pipelines in 40+ countries. As global energy needs rise, pipeline safety becomes non-negotiable, and with Malin Energy, you're not just securing pipelines; you're partnering with a beacon of expertise and reliability in pipeline security.
